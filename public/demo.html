Common Questions

Am I compliant right now? ▾
Enter your company number above and we'll tell you within 30 seconds — no account needed. We pull live data directly from Companies House.
What happens if I miss a deadline? ▾
Companies House issues automatic penalties for late accounts — from £150 for up to one month late, rising to £1,500 for more than six months. Confirmation statement failures can lead to prosecution. FineGuard ensures you never reach that point.
Can you monitor multiple companies? ▾
Yes. Contact us to discuss multi-company monitoring. We'll arrange a plan that works for your portfolio.
Can my accountant use FineGuard? ▾
Yes. Your accountant can be added as an authorised user. They'll see the same status and receive the same alerts.
What if my company is already overdue? ▾
Don't panic. Enter your company number and we'll show you exactly where things stand. If you're overdue, a real member of our team will contact you directly to help you understand your options.
